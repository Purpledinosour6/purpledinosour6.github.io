Fun

With Emmit Vision you can have the fun of a scavenger hunt, but not have to leave your house. Scavenger hunts allow kids to practice problem-solving in a tangible way. It helps to reinforce and act-out methods they have been taught by parents or teachers in a physical way.

Caution

Kids can get hyper so its good to make sure they are all on leashes and the house is properly padded for the safty of the children during the hunt, if leashes are not availble to you just make sure to remind the children to not look a thier phones while running.
